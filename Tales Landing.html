The prompt
Generated by Gemini · 15-second shot script
0–4s · Opening

Medium shot on Joe, gently stroking the shimmering mane of a Unicorn in a lush, sun-dappled forest. Warm sunlight filters through watercolor-textured leaves; floating spirits drift peacefully around them.

4–9s · Development

Joe and the Unicorn walk along a winding forest path. The path opens onto a clearing with an ancient tree, its branches adorned with tiny luminous blossoms. The Unicorn nudges his side affectionately.

9–13s · Key moment

The tree shimmers and reveals a hidden archway leading to a magical countryside village, aglow with soft warm light. Whimsical creatures flutter by. Joe steps through, filled with peaceful adventure.

13–15s · Closing

Joe stands with the Unicorn on a gentle rise overlooking the cozy village. The Unicorn nuzzles his shoulder. The frame holds, the dreamy music fades, ending on a feeling of complete wonder.
